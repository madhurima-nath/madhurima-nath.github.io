\item Deployed production solution with automated weekly reports and interactive dashboards enabling executives to filter insights by business unit and monitor emerging themes.

\item Solution received executive recognition and wide organisational adoption for providing actionable intelligence on safety policy and resource allocation.
\end{zitemize}
