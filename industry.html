DATA ENGINEERING

  • Data Migration to Salesforce for a Nonprofit Organisation: 1TB of historical data for accounts donating to a non-profit organisation was migrated to Salesforce. SQL stored procedures and optimised ELTL pipelines in Azure Data Factory. Multiple validation and data quality checks were implemented to reduced migration errors and enhanced overall enterprise-level data management. Read more here.
  • Network Predictive Maintenance Solution for a Telecom Client: A statistical risk score was developed using various radio frequency metrics to monitor network performance of consumer devices and identify at-risk infrastructure before failures occurred across multiple geographical areas in the country. Based on initial analysis, $8M+ savings in annual costs was projected by avoiding reductions of about 30K customer calls and 6K service visits. Details on risk score here.
